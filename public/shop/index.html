FAQ

Good questions, honest answers

Do I need any design skills?+

None at all. The templates open in Canva, which is free and works in your browser. You simply click a text box and type — names, dates, colours and fonts are all easy to change. Each set includes a short how-to.

How do I receive my files?+

Instantly. As soon as checkout is complete, you'll get an email with a private link that copies the templates into your own Canva account. Nothing is posted — it's all digital and yours to keep.

Can I print at home or at a local shop?+

Yes. Every set exports as a print-ready PDF at standard sizes, so you can print at home, at a local print shop, or send digital versions by link. We include the recommended sizes and paper.

Will it work in my country and currency?+

Yes. Prices are shown in Canadian dollars, and checkout displays your local currency and handles any taxes automatically. Because the files are digital, they reach you anywhere in the world.

What if a set isn't right for me?+

You're covered by a 14-day happiness guarantee. If a template doesn't work for your wedding, write to us within 14 days and we'll refund you in full.

Is the VowNook planning app really free?+

Yes — couples plan for free on VowNook, including guest list, budget and the vendor marketplace. The shop is an optional set of finishing touches, not something you need to plan your day.
